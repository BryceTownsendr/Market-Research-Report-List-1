It is convenient and secure, ensuring efficient hotel management. </p><p>Magnetic card locks utilize magnetic stripe cards for entry. They are cost-effective and widely used due to their compatibility with existing systems. </p><p>Smart card locks employ smart cards that store data for access. They provide enhanced security features and allow for more advanced functionality, such as remote monitoring and control.</p></p>
