<p><p>The plastic fans and blowers market is divided into three types based on the material used: FRP, PVC, and PP. FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) fans and blowers are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) fans and blowers are cost-effective and resistant to chemicals, making them commonly used in corrosive environments. PP (Polypropylene) fans and blowers offer excellent chemical resistance and are widely used in industries dealing with acids, bases, and solvents. These different materials cater to the specific needs and requirements of different industries.</p></p>
