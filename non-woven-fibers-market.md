Fluff Pulp is a cellulose-based material used in absorbent cores of hygiene products for moisture retention. Stretch non-woven fibers provide elasticity and are commonly used in the production of surgical drapes and gowns. Polyester and Filaments are synthetic fibers used in various applications including filtration, geotextiles, and automotive industries.</p></p>
