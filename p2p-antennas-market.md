Parabolic Antennas feature a dish-shaped reflector to focus signals, providing long-range and high-gain transmission. Flat Panel Antennas are compact and low-profile antennas that offer a wide beamwidth for shorter distances. Yagi Antennas employ a linear array of dipole elements to achieve high-directional gain with a narrow beamwidth. These antennas find applications in wireless communication systems, offering diverse options based on the required range, gain, and directionality for P2P communication needs.</p></p>
