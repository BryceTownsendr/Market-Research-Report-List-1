<p><p>The optical dichroic filter market encompasses various types of filters. Bandpass filters allow a specific wavelength range to pass through while blocking others. UV-bandpass filters specifically transmit ultraviolet light. Longpass and shortpass filters transmit light with longer or shorter wavelengths, respectively. NIR cut filters block near-infrared light. Notch filters reject a narrow wavelength range. Lastly, there are other types of filters available in the market that cater to specific needs. These filters find applications in various industries such as optics, telecommunications, biomedical imaging, and spectroscopy, among others.</p></p>
