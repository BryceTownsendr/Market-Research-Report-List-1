<p><p>The VOC sensor market refers to the market for volatile organic compound (VOC) sensors used to detect harmful gases in various industries and applications. The market can be segmented into three main types: semiconductor sensors, PID sensors, and other types of sensors. Semiconductor sensors utilize a sensing element to measure changes in resistance when exposed to VOCs. PID sensors utilize a lamp to emit ultraviolet light, causing VOCs to ionize and generate an electrical current. Other types of VOC sensors include metal-oxide sensors, electrochemical sensors, and optical sensors.
