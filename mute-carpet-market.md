<p><p>The mute carpet market consists of various types, including woven, needle felt, knotted, and others. Needle felt carpets are created using a technique where fibers are entangled together to form a dense, flat fabric, offering excellent acoustic properties. Knotted carpets are handcrafted by tying knots in the yarn, resulting in intricate patterns. The "others" category comprises various carpet types that don't fit into the aforementioned classifications, such as tufted or printed carpets. These diverse options cater to different preferences and needs within the mute carpet market.</p></p>
