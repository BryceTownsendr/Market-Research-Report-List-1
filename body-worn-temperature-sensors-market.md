Temperature sensors measure the body temperature, position sensors determine the location of the sensor on the body, and inertial sensors assess the body's acceleration and orientation. Medical-based sensors are designed specifically for medical applications, and image sensors capture visual information. These sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and managing human body temperature.</p></p>
