<p><p>The lithium-ion power battery material market includes four main types of materials: cathode material, anode material, electrolyte, and separator. </p><p>Cathode material is responsible for storing and releasing positive lithium ions during battery operation, while anode material does the same for negative lithium ions. Electrolyte is a conductive material that allows the movement of lithium ions between the cathode and anode. Separator acts as a physical barrier to prevent direct contact between the cathode and anode, ensuring the safe and efficient operation of the battery. These materials play crucial roles in enabling the functioning of lithium-ion power batteries.</p></p>
