<p><p>An Indoor Distributed Antenna System (DAS) refers to a network of antennas used to enhance cellular and wireless coverage within indoor spaces. There are two main types of DAS systems - Active DAS and Passive DAS. </p><p>Active DAS involves the use of active components such as amplifiers and signal processors to boost and distribute the wireless signal throughout the building. It requires power sources and is more complex in terms of installation and maintenance.</p><p>On the other hand, Passive DAS uses passive components like coaxial cables and splitters to distribute the wireless signal. It does not require power sources and is relatively simpler to install and maintain.</p></p>
