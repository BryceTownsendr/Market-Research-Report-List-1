<p><p>Radiation curing adhesive market is divided into two major types: ultraviolet (UV) curing and electron beam (EB) curing. UV curing involves using UV light to initiate a chemical reaction, transforming the adhesive from a liquid to a solid state. It is widely utilized for its fast curing time and ability to cure a variety of substrates. On the other hand, EB curing utilizes a high-energy beam of electrons to initiate the curing process, making it ideal for applications where heat-sensitive substrates are involved. Both technologies offer efficient and reliable adhesive curing solutions.</p></p>
