The market for mass spectrometer is categorized into different types. GC-MS combines gas chromatography with mass spectrometry to analyze volatile compounds. LC-MS uses liquid chromatography for non-volatile compounds. MALDI-TOF identifies biomolecules using laser and measures the time taken for ions to reach the detector. ICP-MS is employed for trace elemental analysis through plasma ionization. The "Others" category includes niche applications like thermal desorption and spark-source MS.</p></p>
