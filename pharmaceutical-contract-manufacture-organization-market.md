<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Contract Manufacturing Organization (CMO) market can be divided into two main types: Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and Finished Dose Formulations (FDFs). APIs refer to the chemical compounds or substances that are biologically active and have medicinal properties. CMOs specializing in APIs are involved in the production of these substances. FDFs, on the other hand, represent the final product ready for consumption or administration, such as tablets, capsules, or injectables. CMOs focusing on FDFs engage in the manufacturing and packaging of these finished pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
